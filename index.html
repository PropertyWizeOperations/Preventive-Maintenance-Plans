Preventative Maintenance

Protecting Your Bottom Line

Stop Unexpected Costs Before They Happen.

Nothing destroys an owner's cash flow faster than a surprise $5,000 HVAC replacement in the dead of winter or a massive roof leak that destroys a tenant's ceiling. Emergency repairs are always more expensive than preventative care.

We created the PropertyWize Preventative Maintenance Program to put you back in control of your expenses. By actively servicing your most critical assets, this program is structured to:

  • Drastically reduce midnight emergency calls.
  • Extend the usable lifespan of your expensive appliances and systems.
  • Ensure consistent, photographic documentation using trusted, approved vendors.

Choose Your Protection Plans

1. HVAC Preventative Maintenance

Investment: $199 per year
Approved Vendor: Brian (Anytime Services) | Includes 2 Visits Per Year

Scope of Work (Spring & Fall Visits):

✔ Replace standard HVAC filter
✔ Thermostat functionality check

✔ Inspect blower motor (visual)
✔ Inspect refrigerant lines (visual)
✔ Inspect outdoor condenser unit
✔ Remove debris from condenser
✔ Flush condensate drain line (if accessible)

Documentation Included:

✔ Service notes
✔ Deficiency notes
✔ Photos

Exclusions (Not Included):

X Refrigerant recharge/leak testing
X Repairs, parts, or replacements
X Electrical work / Emergency service

2. Roof & Gutter Inspection

Investment: $199 – $300 per visit
Approved Vendor: TnT Roofing | Quotes based on roof type & access

Scope of Work:

Gutter Cleaning:
✔ Remove debris from front & rear gutters
✔ Clear accessible downspouts
✔ Basic water flow test (if hose available)

Roof Inspection (Visual / Non-Invasive):
✔ Inspect roof surface (shingles/flat)
✔ Inspect flashing, vents, and chimney
✔ Inspect roof penetrations
✔ Check for soft spots, ponding, deterioration
✔ Identify signs of leaks or potential failure

Documentation Included:

✔ Photo report (8–12 photos)
✔ Written condition summary
✔ Recommendation (Serviceable, Repair Needed, or Replacement Likely)

Exclusions (Not Included):

X Repairs, sealing, or patchwork
X Structural work
X Steep roofs / 3+ story access (quoted separately)

3. Appliance Preventative Maintenance

Approved Vendor: NT Appliance

Scope of Work:

Kitchen & General:
✔ Inspect refrigerator (cooling, seals, leaks)
